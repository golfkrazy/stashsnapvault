StashSnap is a privacy-first inventory app designed to help older adults discreetly 
track hidden valuables and important documents. It emphasizes intuitive design 
and memory joggers to support recall, while safeguarding sensitive information 
through secure, user-controlled access. The app features a clean interface, 
customizable categories, and optional reminders, all tailored to empower 
users with clarity and control. Its thoughtful UX reflects Anaofelia’s 
commitment to resilience, transparency, and celebration of progress — 
making it not just a tool, but a trusted companion for personal organization.
